Chinese Stir Fried Eggs and Tomatoes Recipe

Ingredients 1 teaspoon cornstarch 6 eggs 1 teaspoon salt 1⁄2 teaspoon white pepper 2 tablespoons Shao xing rice wine 1⁄4 cup cooking oil 2 scallions, thinly sliced 2 medium-sized tomatoes or 3 roma tomatoes, roughly chopped 2 tablespoons ketchup 1⁄4 teaspoon sugar Directions In a small bowl, stir cornstarch with 2 tablespoons water. Set aside. In a medium mixing bowl, beat eggs well with salt and white pepper.
